Frequently Asked Questions

Hopefully one of these will provide the answers you're looking for.

  • What is the purpose of this service?

    The purpose of this service is to transfer and deliver prescriptions.

  • Does this service have a cost?

    The cost of prescription will be added as per usual. Only delivery to your choosen address costs a fee.

  • Do I have to have it delivered to an address?

    No, you can choose to have it left at the chemist so that you may pick it up yourself.

  • Is this service only linked to specific chemists and doctors?

    No, this service is open to all doctors and chemists.

  • Is this available outside of Ireland?

    This service is currently only provided within the Republic of Ireland.

  • How do I sign up to work as a driver?

    You must be vetted by the company and have a valid drivers license.
